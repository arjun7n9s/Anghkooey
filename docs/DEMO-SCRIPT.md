# Anghkooey — 90-Second Demo Script

**Tagline:** *Where your stuff remembers you.*

Run this on **desktop browser** (dashboard) + **phone** (voice, scan, locate). Total target: **85–90 seconds**.

---

## Beat 1 — Cold open (0–5s)

> "Anghkooey. A memory layer for your physical stuff."

Show login screen briefly, then sign in.

---

## Beat 2 — Home (5–12s)

> "Print your labels. Stick them on boxes. Speak what's inside."

Show home stats (logged / labels). Don't print live — labels should already be printed.

---

## Beat 3 — Dashboard (12–30s) ★ Design beat

Click **Open dashboard** on the big screen.

> "This is your archive. Every box, every item, where you last put it."

Let stat cards count up. Pause on the grid of box cards (location hints + item previews).

---

## Beat 4 — Voice log (30–50s) ★ Magic beat

Switch to phone (or browser responsive mode).

Scan a QR → hold mic:

> "Old Canon camera, two HDMI cables, the battery charger, and a postcard from Sarah."

Release. Show chips appearing in stagger + transcript quote.

> "Speaking while packing is the right interface."

---

## Beat 5 — Find (50–65s)

Type or say: **"find the old camera"**

Tap **Listen to reply** (optional TTS beat).

Tap the **SpecimenCard** result — show snippet from your own voice log.

> "You don't remember where you put it. Anghkooey does."

---

## Beat 6 — Locate (65–80s)

Tap **Locate**. Sweep camera across printed QR labels.

Gold brackets + **HERE** badge + haptic.

> "Sweep the room. The box you want shows up."

---

## Beat 7 — Share (80–88s) ★ Pitch beat

On a logged box → **Share with partner@email.com**

Open incognito window as partner → scan same QR → they see your items.

> "Share with anyone. They can find anything you stored."

---

## Beat 8 — Close (88–90s)

> "Anghkooey. Where your stuff remembers you."

---

## Fallback plan

| If this breaks | Do this instead |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Voice / mic | Type items on log screen |
| Camera locate | Show pre-recorded 10s clip |
| Share live | Show second browser already logged in as partner |
| Find empty | Use pre-logged Box #14 / Camera gear |
